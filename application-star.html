Star Application
What will you be doing?

Stars help staff members with tasks that don't involve moderation. This mostly involves building structures including spawns and minigames, but also concerns managing minigames. As a star, you will not have any special permissions or obligations on the survival server. However, you'll have full build and worldedit permissions on the lobby server, and will occasionally be helping with event organization.

Please spend some time on your application. The more long and detailed your answers are, the better. Good luck!
