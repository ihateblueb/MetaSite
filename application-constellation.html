Constellation Application
What will you be doing?

As a Constellation, you'll be helping to moderate the server, including both in-game and Discord. You'll be responding to griefs, hacks, and other reported rule-breaks, along with answering questions related to moderation, plugins, and occasionally administration. Constellations are also invited to staff meetings for input into administration and major server decisions.

Please spend some time on your application. The more long and detailed your answers are, the better. Good luck!
