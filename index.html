Welcome

Here's the TL;DR of what the server's about.



Focus
  • Largely oriented around Slimefun, a plugin that adds hundreds of new items
  • Design huge factories, automate everything, become invincible, go to space, build cool stuff
  • Powerful server hardware allows for large-scale factories and machines without impacting performance

Transparency
  • A full list of plugins and Slimefun addons can be found here
  • A changelog and roadmap can be found here
  • A log of all warnings and bans handed out can be found on our Discord.

Ranks
  • An overview of ranks can be found in-game using /rank
  • Craft increasingly difficult Slimefun items to rank up
  • Ranking up allows you to use new commands and abilities (eg: using colours in chat)

Events
  • We regularly host events, such as finding armor stand scenes scattered around spawn
  • If it's your sort of thing, we have a minigame world for you to murder your friends/enemies in

Sounds good to you? Drop by using obelisk.game-server.cc or join our Discord.
