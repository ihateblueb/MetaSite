WELCOME


SLIMEFUN

Slimefun adds 500+ new items, no downloads required. Design huge factories, become invincible, go to space, build cool stuff. The end goal? Automate everything.


TRANSPARENCY

A changelog and roadmap can be found on Discord. A full list of addons and plugins can be found in-game with /plugins and /sf versions.



RANKS

Rank up to unlock new commands and abilities, such as coloured chat. Craft increasingly difficult Slimefun items to rank up. Find out more by using /rank in-game.



EVENTS

Events every week, both PvP & non PvP, with prizes. Murder your frenemies in our custom minigames world.



HARDWARE

16 cores | 4.7 GHz | 128 GB
We place very few restrictions on logistics or energy systems. Automate to your heart's content.



ADDONS

Play with the latest Slimefun addons, including Infinity Expansion, Networks, Galactifun, SlimeTinker, and many more.



MAINTENANCE

The server is consistently maintained and updated to keep content interesting. As of 09/08/2022, we've fixed 68 issues. A full changelog can be found on Discord.
