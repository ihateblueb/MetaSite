Staff ranks
Galaxy

Galaxies assist the Universe (Idra) in maintaining the server. They have access to console and can use every command on the server, as well as having access to server files. Galaxies also share the duties of Constellations, though to a lesser extent.

Constellation

Constellations act as moderators, preventing the server from becoming too chaotic and handling rule breaks such as griefs. They are equipped with permissions to use CoreProtect, can override GriefPrevention, and are able to hand out warnings as well as other consequences where necessary.

Star

Stars help to design and build server structures and events. They have permissions that let them build in the lobby server, including worldedit permissions. Stars are organised by Aria_Night who manages events and determines structures to be built.
