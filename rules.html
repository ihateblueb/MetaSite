RULES
  • No griefing or stealing, including unclaimed areas. If you give someone permission to access your claims, you are responsible for the consequences.
  • PVP is disabled by default. You are responsible for the consequences of enabling it.
  • NSFW/discriminatory content and flaming is disallowed. If you must argue with someone, do it in private messages.
  • Try not to spam or overuse capslock. Try not to break up messages onto multiple lines.
  • If you wish to advertise anything unrelated to the server, contact a staff member.
  • Cheats/hacks such as X-ray are disallowed.
  • Exploiting a bug will result in a ban. Reporting exploits you discover on Discord will result in a reward.
  • Bedrock breaking is allowed. TNT duping is allowed, but duping any other item is not.
  • Autoclickers and macros are allowed, but auto-building (eg: schematica's printer mode) is not.
  • Try not to enforce rules yourself; notify a staff member if there's an issue.
  • Please keep global chat English only.
  • And finally: Use common sense! There will likely be loopholes in these rules. We do reserve the right to warn you for exploiting them. The rules are subject to change at any time.
